Speaker Introduction

The Lord has given Rev. Dr. Joe Madhu & Rev. Dr. Jessie Veena the Vision of “The Calvary Love of Jesus Ministries”. Founded on the Word of God it is a registered non-profit Christian service organization. Before they were born, their parents received a prophecy. This was confirmed by the vision of our Lord Jesus Christ to Rev. Dr. Joe Madhu & Rev. Dr. Jessie Veena at a small age of 3 years and 6 months, calling them into HIS ministry. For more than Four decades right from their mid-teens, The Holy Spirit is using Rev. Dr. Joe Madhu & Rev. Dr. Jessie Veena across the globe to spread HIS message of Love, Compassion & Healing leading unto the Salvation of the soul. Millions are being saved by the Lord worldwide through these humble and anointed servants.
